Child Labor Project

Child Labor Poem

Scarred by the hands because of all of their hard work
Tiny fingers dirtied with mud and murky water
Their masters telling them to work even harder
Treated like some type of objects
Looking as if they are created projects

The rusty buckets that hook onto their fingertips
All you can hear is the soft footsteps
Groaning everytime they bend down
Getting lower and lower to the floor until they cant feel their feet and all they can do is bow
The soft breeze filled with smoke
Kids looking as if they grew up being broke
Without ever given a second chance
These kids only get one last glance

Social Media Project

Intro To Project

For this project, we had to research a certain topic that had to do with social media. For my project personally, i researched social media and race. I read many articles about racism on social media such as Twitter. The reason I chose this topic specifaclly is because I feel as if it isn't talked about enough and should be discussed more at schools and all around in general. I decided to construct my art piece by putting the racist tweets as my backgorund and puttin gthe diversity hands in the middle symbolizing that we are all one whole.

Social Media Poem: Unite

Left, lost, and lonely
When all people of color want is to just be holy
Pictures and posts about black people being monkeys
People treating us as little punkies
Make this world a better place
Keep that stuff to yourself and say that in your own space

Unfair, mistreated, and speechless
As everyone just watches eating peaches
Tired of the shit that people have to say
When all black people want is to say hey
Why can’t we just be treated with love
Instead of being dirtied like a glove
Why can’t we just make a song together
Why can’t we just go to the mall together
