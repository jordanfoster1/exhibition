Process of Creating 3-D Model

There were steps to creating this masterpiece of a model. First we sketched out what the actual model would look like and how would we want to design it. Then we discussed what materials were needed to actually construct the model. We went out to get supplies and decided to put the model on a little farm because it gave it sort of that 19th century, Industrial Revolution feel. We hot glued everything together and had it being pulled by a horse because that is what they used to pull the threshing machine. Finally, we added little mini affects with the grass and the gardening tools kind of laying around the farm.
